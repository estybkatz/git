    About Us

    Pic-Up: An Online Marketplace for Exceptional Photography

    Welcome to our online marketplace for photography enthusiasts. Our platform connects buyers and sellers of high-quality pictures from around the world, providing a secure and convenient way to discover and sell exceptional images.
    As a buyer, you can browse our extensive selection of images, from breathtaking landscapes to captivating portraits and abstract art. Our user-friendly interface and advanced search options make it easy to find exactly what you're looking for, while our secure payment system ensures a seamless and safe purchasing experience.
    As a seller, you can showcase your portfolio and sell your pictures to a global audience. Our platform offers a range of tools and resources to help you set prices, connect with buyers, and grow your business. We also provide competitive fees and a secure payment system, so you can focus on creating and selling your art.
    Our online marketplace is designed to meet the needs of photography enthusiasts, whether you're a professional photographer or an amateur looking to buy or sell pictures. Join our community today and discover a world of exceptional images.

    Getting Started: A Guide to Using Our Photography Marketplace

    Upload A Photo

    Only users registered as business clients may upload a photo.
    Once you register as a business client, you can login and add a button using the add pic button, and upload the photo you want to sell

    The viewing options

    On the left side of the screen, we have a set of icons, which can be used to navigate the website.

    List view

    The first button brings all the available pictures as a list, with their url's and prices, and editing options.



    Gallery

    Pressing the button, will open the gallery mode, in which you can see all the pictures, as cards simultaneously.



    Carousel View

    pressing the button, open a carousel view of the pictures. In the Carousel mode you can toggle the pictures using the arrows.

    Sort the list

    You can use the and buttons to sort the pictures in an ascending and descending order, of the titles.

    Search a picture

    You can enter a word, and it will search for a picture starting with it.

    Additional details of a picture

    You can press any picture, when viewing as a List or as a Gallery,and additional details about it will pop up.

    Editing and deleting a picture

    Pressing the button allows you to edit a picture, and the allows you to delete it.
